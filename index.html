About Me

Academic Training

I was introduced to web development as part of Telecom's "Digitalers" program. I already had experience in Algorithms and Data Structures due to my first years in the career of Information Systems Engineering (National Technological University)

In 2021 I decided to dedicate completely to that area, using my knowledge as a .NET Developer acquired in "EducacionIT" and other technologies that I was trained with through asynchronous courses (Udemy, Coursera, etc.)

Why Web Development?

I decided to form in web development because there are currently many projects in process of emerging that don't have enough visibility due to their lack of Internet presence.

It is motivating and rewarding to be able to contribute to the success of great ideas.

Present

I'm perfecting myself in technologies already seen, learning about .NET Core and design patterns at "EducacionIT", thanks to the scholarship that was awarded to me at the end of the C # .NET Developer courses, in the year 2020.
